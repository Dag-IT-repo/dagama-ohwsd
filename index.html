ABOUT

DA GAMA Occupational Health, Wellness, Safety and Disability (OHWSD) Services is a Private Company founded by FC Marince in 2015. The company is based and operate mainly in (but not restricted to) the greater Johannesburg area. DA GAMA is a 100% Black Female owned company striving to raise awareness in organisations to eliminate, reduce and or manage the risk of OHS prescribed by the OHS Act 85/1993.

By doing so, DA GAMA aims to:

  • Reduce and manage:
    • Health costs
    • Absenteeism
  • Enhance and Increase:
    • Productivity
    • Morale
    • Employee Retention
VISION

To implement an integrated healthcare solution in the workplace. To continuously develop and improve the wellbeing of the Employee by eradicating any health and safety risks. This will be done in collaboration with all stakeholders to meet the unique needs of each work environment in a spirit of cooperation, trust and partnership.


MISSION

Provide excellent healthcare and safety services through planning and coordination to ensure a healthy workforce and a safe working environment.

Feya's Profile

Feya completed her Formal Higher Education and Training at Lebone College in 1988 acquiring her Diploma in General Nursing. In 1989 she acquired her Diploma Midwifery after attending Coronationville Nursing College.
Feya furthered her schooling at Wits Technikon between 2000-2001 and acquired another BTech in Occupational Health and a Diploma in Nursing Management.
During 2010, studying at UNISA Feya acquired her National Diploma Safety Management and in 2014 her BTech Safety Management. Currently Feya is in the process of completing her Dispensing license at MEDUNSA.
