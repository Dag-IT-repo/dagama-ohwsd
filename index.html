ABOUT

DA GAMA Occupational Health, Wellness, Safety and Disability (OHWSD) Services is a Private Company founded by FC Marince in 2015. The company is based and operates mainly in, but not restricted to the greater Johannesburg area. Dagama OHWSD strives to raise awareness in organisations, to eliminate; reduce and/or manage the risk of OHS prescribed by the OHS Act 85/1993.

By doing so, DA GAMA aims to:

  • Reduce and manage:
    • Health costs
    • Absenteeism
  • Enhance and Increase:
    • Productivity
    • Morale
    • Employee Retention
VISION

To implement an integrated healthcare solution in the workplace. To continuously develop and improve the wellbeing of the Employee by eradicating any health and safety risks. This will be done in collaboration with all stakeholders to meet the unique needs of each work environment in a spirit of cooperation, trust and partnership.

MISSION

Provide excellent healthcare and safety services through planning and coordination to ensure a healthy workforce and a safe working environment.
